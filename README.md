**Super Mario 64 Ultimate** is a fan‑made project inspired by SM64 Coop DX. Development will take time since I am a solo dev. Enjoy!

In order to play, you must provide your own .z64 Super Mario 64 ROM. However, due to legal reasons, I’m not allowed to tell you where to get one. Sorry about that!
